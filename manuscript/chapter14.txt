# Communication


## Sending audio messages

### Problem
You want to send audio messages with your Mac.

### Solution
1.  Open the Messages App and start a conversation.

2.  On the right bottom of the window you will see a microphone. Click it to start recording.

3.  When you are done with your audio message just click the stop button on the right.

4.  Now you can choose between `Send` to send the message to the people in the conversation and `Cancel` to discard the message.
